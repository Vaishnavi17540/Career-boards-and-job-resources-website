Here’s a list of 10 different career options, with brief descriptions, required degrees, pay ranges, and top institutions for studying:

1. Molecular Engineering
Degree Needed: PhD in Molecular Engineering
What It Does: Focuses on the application of engineering principles to molecular biology and materials science to design and develop new materials.
Pay Range: ₹7,00,000 - ₹20,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Stanford University, National Institute of Technology (India)

2. Software Engineer
Degree Needed: Bachelor's in Computer Science or Software Engineering
What It Does: Develops, tests, and maintains software applications and systems.
Pay Range: ₹6,00,000 - ₹15,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), Carnegie Mellon University, University of California, Berkeley

3. Data Scientist
Degree Needed: Master's in Data Science, Computer Science, or Statistics
What It Does: Analyzes and interprets complex data to help companies make decisions.
Pay Range: ₹8,00,000 - ₹18,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Stanford University, Harvard University

4. Research Scientist (Biotechnology)
Degree Needed: PhD in Biotechnology or Molecular Biology
What It Does: Conducts research to discover new biological processes, drugs, and technologies.
Pay Range: ₹6,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Indian Institute of Technology (IITs), University of Cambridge, California Institute of Technology

5. AI/ML Engineer
Degree Needed: Bachelor's or Master's in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, or Robotics
What It Does: Designs and develops machine learning models and AI systems to automate tasks.
Pay Range: ₹8,00,000 - ₹20,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Stanford University, IIT Bombay, University of Toronto

6. Chemical Engineer
Degree Needed: Bachelor's in Chemical Engineering
What It Does: Works on the design and optimization of processes that convert raw materials into valuable products.
Pay Range: ₹6,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: IIT Madras, University of California, Berkeley

7. Civil Engineer
Degree Needed: Bachelor's in Civil Engineering
What It Does: Plans, designs, and oversees the construction of infrastructure like buildings, roads, and bridges.
Pay Range: ₹5,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: IIT Roorkee, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

8. Digital Marketing Specialist
Degree Needed: Bachelor's in Marketing, Business, or Communications
What It Does: Uses online platforms to create strategies for promoting products and services.
Pay Range: ₹4,00,000 - ₹10,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Indian School of Business (ISB), University of California, Berkeley

9. Healthcare Consultant
Degree Needed: MBA or Master's in Healthcare Management
What It Does: Provides expert advice on improving healthcare organizations and services.
Pay Range: ₹8,00,000 - ₹20,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: Harvard University, University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA)

10. Mechanical Engineer
Degree Needed: Bachelor's or Master's in Mechanical Engineering
What It Does: Designs and develops mechanical systems and devices for various industries.
Pay Range: ₹5,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per year
Top Institutions: IIT Bombay, University of Michigan, Georgia Tech
